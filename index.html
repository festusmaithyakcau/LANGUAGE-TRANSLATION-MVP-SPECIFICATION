About

Introduction

We are three individuals who experienced childhood in a multicultural nation and each has an alternate primary language. Notwithstanding, it was an occasion that united individuals from everywhere the world and touched off our common energy for separating language boundaries. Read more...

The Spark

At this occasion, we experienced issues speaking with non-English speakers. This horrendous language obstruction experience was a defining moment for us all.

The Epiphany

We understand that language isn't just a method for correspondence yet additionally an extension to comprehend and interface with individuals from varying backgrounds. Together, we needed to make an answer that makes this language more open and advances intercultural understanding.

Project's Birth

At the point when we began our venture project for Holberton, we began exploring language interpretation devices. We imagined a help that deciphers words, yet in addition catches social subtleties, making it simpler for individuals to interface and see one another. We chose to consolidate our special abilities and experience to make this vision a reality.

Conclusion

This task is our cooperative work to separate language splits and cultivate associations between individuals. It is a celebration of the power of language and its capacity to unite people regardless of their language or origin.
