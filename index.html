Features

Translation Services

Easily translate text from one language to another with our advanced translation algorithms.

Language Detection

Our AI-powered tool can automatically detect the language of a selected text, saving you time.

Location Detection

When location is enabled, our web app can automatically detect the location and and ouput weather information of the area.

About

Introduction

We are three individuals who experienced childhood in a multicultural nation and alternate primary language. Notwithstanding, it was an occasion that united individuals from everywhere the world. We decided to expound on more areas that can promote unity in language and came up with the solution weather translation . Read more...

The Spark

At this occasion, we noticed that many people have interest in wether conditions of both their areas and different areas.

The Epiphany

We understand that language should not be the barrier to exploring and getting to know different weather conditions.

Project's Birth

At the point when we began our venture project for Holberton, we began exploring language interpretation devices. We imagined a help that deciphers weather information into different languages. We chose to consolidate our special abilities and experience to make this vision a reality.

Conclusion

This task is our cooperative work to ensure people fulfil their interest in getting to know the weather conditions of different places regardless of their location
