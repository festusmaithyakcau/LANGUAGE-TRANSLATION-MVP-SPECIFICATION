About

Where it all began

The inspiration for the weather forecasting project, which we excited to present as a Portfolio Project for Holberton School, stems from a simple, yet impactful local experience. Read more...


Living in a diverse community where multiple languages are spoken, we often noticed that some residents, particularly those who were not fluent in the local language, struggled to access crucial weather information. One incident where a neighbor, who primarily spoke French, was caught off guard by a sudden rainstorm because they couldn't understand the local weather forecast.


This incident struck a chord with me, and we realized that there was a genuine need for a weather forecasting tool that could bridge this language gap. It was a moment of clarity that inspired me to develop a weather forecasting application that not only delivers accurate predictions but also translates them into multiple languages, ensuring that everyone in our diverse community can stay informed about the weather.


As a team, we dedicated ourselves to learning the necessary skills and working on this project, turning a local observation into a solution that benefits my community and beyond. It's not just a project for school; it's a contribution that aims to make a positive impact on people's everyday lives, helping them plan their day with ease, regardless of the language they speak.
